Real, Virtual & the Challenge:

The only Real things in this world are the protons, electrons & neutrons which combine to form an atom. Everything built on atoms is real. Everything else is virtual (artificial).

You, me, your parents, rivers are all real. To support an equilibrium between all the real things, Humans have found a need in history to form virtual things. Virtual/ Artificial things are - Nations, Consitution, Laws, Markets, Companies, Marriage, Currency, Religion, etc.

Not all artificial things made in the past are relevant at this time. Not all the virtual things made today would be relevant in the future. We can challenge all the virtual things made in the past today. Someone will challenge all the virtual things which are present today.

That someone is the challenger, the entrepreneur, the hero.

Something funny - In this world, humans have found a need to create another virtual world - Video Game and Metaverse.
